# PowerBI-Market-Analysis
📊 Market Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

Excited to share my latest data analytics project built in Power BI, focused on exploring sales performance and profitability across different market segments.

📊 The dashboard highlights several important perspectives:
• Sales & Profit by Category – Technology leads in profitability, while Furniture shows lower margins.
• Profit Ratio by State – Geographic comparison to identify high- and low-performing markets.
• Sales Trends by Segment – Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments analyzed over time to understand demand patterns.

🎯 Purpose of the Dashboard:
To transform raw sales data into actionable insights that support better business decisions, performance tracking, and strategic planning.

Power BI • Data Modeling • Data Visualization • Business Analysis • Dashboard Design
